# Design System Document

## Theme Overview

This document outlines the core design principles and token values that define our digital products' aesthetic and functional aspects. The theme is designed to be expressive and modern, focusing on clarity and user engagement.

## Color Palette

Our color palette is built around a vibrant and inviting scheme.

*   **Primary Color:** `#70A1FF` - A bright, friendly blue, used for primary calls to action, important headings, and interactive elements.
*   **Secondary Color:** `#FFB347` - A warm, inviting orange, complementing the primary color and used for secondary actions, highlights, and status indicators.
*   **Tertiary Color:** `#FFCCBC` - A soft, delicate peach, offering an additional accent for decorative elements, badges, or subtle background variations.
*   **Neutral Color:** `#F5F5F7` - A very light, almost white grey, providing a clean and versatile background for content and UI surfaces.

The current color mode is **light**, providing a bright and airy interface experience.

## Typography

We utilize a carefully selected set of fonts to ensure readability and maintain a consistent brand voice.

*   **Headlines:** `plusJakartaSans` - Used for all major headings and titles, providing a modern and clean aesthetic.
*   **Body Text:** `manrope` - Chosen for its excellent readability across various screen sizes and contexts, used for paragraphs, descriptions, and general content.
*   **Labels:** `manrope` - Consistent with body text for clarity and uniformity in form labels, buttons, and smaller informational text.

## Shape and Form

The aesthetic emphasizes a distinct shape language.

*   **Roundedness:** `3` (Maximum, pill-shaped) - UI elements feature deeply rounded corners, giving a soft, friendly, and approachable feel to all components.

## Spacing

Spacing is critical for readability and visual hierarchy.

*   **Spacing:** `2` (Normal) - A balanced approach to whitespace, providing comfortable separation between elements without feeling too dense or too sparse.
